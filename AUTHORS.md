# GDMMORPG Contributors

**GDMMORPG** is an open-source MMORPG developed collaboratively by a community of contributors from many disciplines — including programming, art, design, sound, writing, and documentation.

Contributions come in many forms: code, textures, sounds, models, animations, gameplay systems, bug reports, documentation, translations, and community support.  
Everyone who helps shape the project is valued equally.

To keep this list meaningful and curated, we include contributors who have made **5 or more commits** across any of the organization’s repositories.  
For the full contribution history, please refer to the Git logs.
